Ceiling Storage Dome

3.007 Introduction to Design

Storage concept for diminishing household space

This concept utilises the dead space above our homes, the ceilings, to create an aesthetically yet functional space to store our household items. Our precedents involved largely around Singapore’s near future, during the time when Population White Paper was instated. This curved, slightly dome shaped structure is custom-fitted on the ceiling with storage boxes that hang from above. These boxes operate individually and are organised by an application which manages the inventory and location of items. The application’s prototype can be seen here: http://bit.ly/storageathome
