Hand-launch Glider

Aerodynamics Project

Designed for furthest range

This goal of this project was to fabricate a hand launch glider to achieve the furthest glide range. Researching methods and designs used by existing hobbyists, we selected Balsa and Carbon Fibre materials by laser cutting a precise airfoil designed for a balanced and well-stabilised flight. Our glider achieved the furthest glide range amongst other groups, reaching 45 metres from a launch height of three storeys
