Bipolar Diathermy Forceps

Biomedical Product Design

Functional redesign

This project was done in partnership with Shuyou Surgical, a Chinese Biomedical company which focuses on developing low-cost surgical tools for use in various medical fields. The redesigned product was termed Swiss Army Diathermy Forceps, and has several features aimed at improving ergonomics and user control. Precedents from this design comes from other similar products by Bowa(Germany) and Covidien(USA). With greater control over the front tips, surgeons can work with better precision and lesser hand fatigue.
